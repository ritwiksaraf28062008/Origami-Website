Interesting Facts About Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • There are over 300 different species of pigeons that can be found throughout the world.

Interesting Facts About Teddy Bear

  1. The term bear-hug was first recorded in 1846.
  2. The Teddy Bears' Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step.
  3. In 1902 in Germany, Steiff launched “Bear 55 PB”, the first toy bear with jointed arms and legs.

Interesting Facts About Camel

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.
  • They bring up the contents of their stomach, along with saliva, and project it out.

Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are a very unique branch of the lizard group of reptiles.
  2. There are around 160 species of chameleon.
  3. Chameleons live in warm varied habitats from rainforests through to deserts.

Interesting Facts About Flying Cicada

  • Cicadas think the sounds made by power tools and lawn maintenance equipment are made by cicadas.
  • Most 17 Year Cicadas have red eyes, but they can also have white, gray, blue , or multi-colored eyes
  • People call these cicadas “locusts” but they are not true locusts — real locusts look like grasshoppers.

Interesting Facts About Panda

  1. A giant panda is much bigger than your teddy bear.
  2. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  3. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.
